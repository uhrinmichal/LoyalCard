Support

For questions or help with LoyalCard, contact Michal Uhrin at uhrin.michal@gmail.com.

Supported card formats

  • QR codes containing 1 to 32 numeric digits
  • EAN-13 codes containing 12 or 13 digits

Data storage

Card names and numbers are stored locally on the watch. LoyalCard does not require an account or an internet connection.
