# LoyalCard Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

LoyalCard is an app for Huawei wearable devices that lets users create and
display loyalty cards in QR and EAN-13 formats.

## Data processing

The app does not collect, transmit, or share personal data with the developer
or third parties. Card names and numbers entered by the user are stored only
in the watch's local system storage.

## Internet and analytics

The app does not require an internet connection, does not use analytics or
advertising services, and does not create a user account.

## Data retention and deletion

Data remains on the watch until the user deletes it in the app, clears the
app's data, or uninstalls the app.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children or other
users.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's data processing practices change, this page and its effective
date will be updated.
