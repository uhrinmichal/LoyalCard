# LoyalCard - AppGallery listing (EN)

## Name

LoyalCard

## Short description

Keep your loyalty QR and EAN-13 cards on your Huawei watch.

## Description

LoyalCard keeps your loyalty cards directly on your watch, so they are ready
when you need them without searching for a plastic card or your phone.

Create up to five custom cards, give each card a name, and choose either QR or
EAN-13 format. You can edit or delete cards and move your most frequently used
card to the first position. Fullscreen scan mode makes the code easier to scan
at checkout.

Card data is stored locally on the watch. The app does not require an account
or an internet connection.

## Key features

- up to 5 custom loyalty cards
- QR codes containing numeric data
- EAN-13 codes using 12 or 13 digits
- add, rename, edit, and delete cards
- move a favorite card to the first position
- fullscreen scan mode
- local storage on the watch
